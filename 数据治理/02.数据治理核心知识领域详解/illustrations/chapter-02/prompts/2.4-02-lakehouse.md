Data Lakehouse Architecture

A unified architecture diagram showing the "Lakehouse" concept.
- Base Layer: "Data Lake Storage" (S3/OSS) - Cheap, vast storage.
- Middle Layer: "Open Table Formats" (Iceberg/Hudi/Delta) - The metadata layer adding structure.
- Top Layer: "Compute Engine" (Spark/Presto/Flink) - Accessing the data.
- Visual Metaphor: A house built on a lake, or a fusion of a warehouse building and a lake.
- Labels in Chinese: "湖仓一体 (Lakehouse)", "开放表格格式", "对象存储".
- Visual Style: Retro architectural drawing.
