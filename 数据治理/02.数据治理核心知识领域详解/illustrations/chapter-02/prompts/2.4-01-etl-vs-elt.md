ETL vs ELT Architecture Comparison

A split-screen comparison of ETL and ELT workflows.
- Top (ETL): Source -> Extract -> Transform (ETL Server) -> Load -> Warehouse.
    - Highlight: Bottleneck at Transform server.
- Bottom (ELT): Source -> Extract -> Load -> Warehouse/Lake (Transform here).
    - Highlight: Scalable transformation inside the warehouse/lake.
