Data Architecture Alignment Pyramid

A three-layered pyramid structure illustrating the alignment of Business, Data, and Technology architectures.
- Top Layer (Gold/Yellow): Business Architecture (Why & Who) - Represented by strategic icons, process flows, and business goals.
- Middle Layer (Teal/Green): Data Architecture (What) - Represented by data models, ER diagrams, and data flows. Connecting business needs to tech implementation.
- Bottom Layer (Blue/Grey): Technology Architecture (How & Where) - Represented by database icons, servers, and cloud infrastructure.
- Visual Style: Retro blueprint style with textured paper background. 
- Labels in Chinese: "业务架构 (Business)", "数据架构 (Data)", "技术架构 (Technology)".
- Arrows indicating alignment from top to bottom.
